    So what does Amiga’s rise and fall mean for today’s electronics world?From a public perspective, Amiga is an outdated, failed work, because its function is far outdated and cannot be accepted by everyone, but for media archaeology, Amiga is a witness to computer history.Although in name, Amiga has become a dead media, what it left has always affected the present world. For example, its color processing system has been extended and used by Microsoft and Apple, and its game platform has become the predecessor of Steam and Wegame.

    Just as in the times, people's definition of demand is also advancing with the times. Perhaps more than a decade ago, computers were not popular. On the game of operation, the answer is no.This is reasonable because the media world is not so friendly. And it itself has become the existence of a living dead.

    Let's take another example. How to judge the Amiga game on the Amiga system and the Steam game platform on the Windows system?The latter is naturally familiar, and the former has to be said to be quite successful as a game platform of the first generation.
